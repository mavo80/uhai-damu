ℹ️ About Uhai Damu

Learn about our mission, vision, and commitment to saving lives through blood donation in Kenya.

Our Mission: Saving Lives Through Technology

Uhai Damu is Kenya's first real-time blood donation network, connecting voluntary donors with hospitals to ensure no life is lost due to blood shortage. We leverage technology to create a responsive, efficient, and life-saving blood supply chain.

🎯

Our Mission

To eliminate preventable deaths caused by blood shortages in Kenya by creating a seamless connection between blood donors and healthcare facilities through innovative technology.

👁️

Our Vision

A Kenya where every patient in need has immediate access to safe, compatible blood through a nationwide network of voluntary donors and efficient distribution systems.

💡

Our Innovation

Real-time SMS alerts, live stock monitoring, and intelligent matching algorithms that connect the right donor with the right patient at the right time.

🤝

Our Partnership

Official partner with Kenya National Blood Transfusion Service (KNBTS), working together to strengthen the national blood supply system.

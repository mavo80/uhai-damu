‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often can I donate blood?

A: Men can donate every 3 months (4 times/year). Women can donate every 4 months (3 times/year).

Q: Does it hurt?

A: You'll feel a quick pinch when the needle is inserted, but most donors report minimal discomfort. The entire donation process is painless.

Q: How long does it take to recover?

A: Your body replaces the fluid within 24 hours and red blood cells within 4-6 weeks. Most donors feel fine after 15 minutes of rest.

Q: Can I donate if I have a medical condition?

A: It depends on the condition. Common conditions like high blood pressure (controlled) and diabetes (controlled) may be eligible. Contact us for specific questions.

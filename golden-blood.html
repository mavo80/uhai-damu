✨ What Makes Golden Blood So Special?

Rh-null blood lacks ALL 61 Rh antigens, making it the most versatile blood type in existence. It can be donated to anyone with any Rh blood type, making it the true "universal donor" for rare blood patients.

✅ Can Donate To:

Anyone with any Rh blood type

The ultimate universal donor for rare cases

❌ Can Receive From:

Rh-null only

This is why Rh-null donors are so precious

⚠️ The Challenge with Golden Blood

🔴

Extreme Rarity

With only 50 known donors worldwide, finding compatible blood for Rh-null patients is incredibly difficult. When they need transfusions, blood often must be shipped internationally.

💉

Donor Dependency

Rh-null individuals are encouraged to donate blood regularly to maintain a global supply for themselves and others with this rare type.

🌏

International Registry

Rh-null donors are registered in global databases to help coordinate donations across countries when emergencies arise.

💎 Do You Have Golden Blood?

If you've been told you have Rh-null or a rare Rh variant, please contact us immediately. Your blood is literally priceless.

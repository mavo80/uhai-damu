ℹ️ About Live Blood Stock Monitoring

Why Live Stock Matters: Blood has a limited shelf life — red blood cells last 42 days, platelets only 5 days, and plasma up to 1 year. Real-time monitoring ensures that hospitals can quickly identify shortages and request donations before critical levels are reached.

How It Works: Our system connects directly with hospital blood banks in Nairobi and Kiambu counties. When blood is used or new donations arrive, the stock levels are updated instantly.

üìã Introduction

Uhai Damu ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our blood donation platform.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy and Kenya's Data Protection Act (2019).

üìä Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, date of birth, gender
  • Contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Location data (county, constituency)
  • Blood type and medical history relevant to donation
  • Donation history and dates

Usage Information:

  • Login credentials and authentication data
  • Device information and IP addresses
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Communication preferences

üîç How We Use Your Information

  • Matching Donors: To connect blood donors with hospitals based on blood type and location
  • Emergency Alerts: To send SMS notifications when blood is needed in your area
  • Account Management: To maintain your donor profile and donation history
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improvement: To enhance our services and develop new features

ü§ù Information Sharing

We only share your information with explicit consent:

  • Hospitals: Your contact details are only shared with hospitals after you confirm availability for a specific donation request
  • KNBTS: Aggregated data shared with Kenya National Blood Transfusion Service for national planning
  • SMS Providers: Phone numbers shared with SMS gateway providers for sending alerts (encrypted)

We never share:

  • Personal information with third-party marketers
  • Medical details beyond what's necessary for donation
  • Information with unauthorized parties

üîê Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access Control: Strict role-based access to donor information
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates
  • Data Minimization: We only collect information necessary for our services

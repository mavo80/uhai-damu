🔬 Why Rare Blood Types Matter

Patients with rare blood types face unique challenges. When they need a transfusion, finding compatible blood can be difficult and time-consuming. For some rare types, there may be fewer than 100 compatible donors in the entire country.

🎯 Challenges for Rare Blood Donors:

  • Limited donor pool when they need blood
  • May need to travel long distances to donate
  • Hospitals must maintain specialized inventories
  • Emergency situations require advanced planning

🎗️ Are You a Rare Blood Donor?

If you have AB negative, B negative, or any rare blood type, your donations are especially valuable. One donation can save multiple lives!
